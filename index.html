Our Story

Four generations.
One obsession.

In 1964, the Goyal family began their journey in tea. Sixty years later, Keshav Goyal carries that legacy forward as the fourth generation. Lush Greens sources directly from the finest tea gardens of Darjeeling and Assam — no single estate, no intermediaries. We pick the best from every harvest, blend with premium natural spices and flowers, and deliver an experience that is clean, honest, and extraordinary.
